---
title: DefaultTextStyle
---

The text style to apply to descendant Text widgets without explicit style.


## 示例

```dart liveslice=DefaultTextStyle
DefaultTextStyle(
  style: TextStyle(
    fontSize: 60,
    color: Colors.blue,
    fontWeight: FontWeight.bold,
  ),
  child: Text('Flutter'),
)
```
