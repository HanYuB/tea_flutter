title: TimePicker

Shows a dialog containing a material design time picker.

## 示例

```dart liveslice=TimePicker height=400px
Future<TimeOfDay> selectedTime = showTimePicker(
  initialTime: TimeOfDay.now(),
  context: context,
);
```
