---
title: Placeholder
---

A widget that draws a box that represents where other widgets will one day be added.

## 示例

```dart liveslice=Placeholder
Container(
  width: 200,
  height: 200,
  color: Colors.white,
  child: Placeholder(
    color: Colors.blue[50],
  ),
)
```
