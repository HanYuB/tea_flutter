---
title: Divider
---

A one device pixel thick horizontal line, with padding on either side.

## 示例

```dart liveslice=Divider
Column(
  mainAxisSize: MainAxisSize.min,
  children: <Widget>[
    ListTile(
      title: Text('Item1')
    ),
    Divider(),
    ListTile(
      title: Text('Item2'),
    ),
    Divider(),
    ListTile(
      title: Text('Item3'),
    ),
  ],
)
```
