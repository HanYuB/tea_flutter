---
title: VerticalDivider
---

A one device pixel thick vertical line, with padding on either side.

## Example

```dart liveslice=VerticalDivider
Row(
  mainAxisSize: MainAxisSize.min,
  children: <Widget>[
    Container(
      child: Text('Item1')
    ),
    VerticalDivider(),
    Container(
      child: Text('Item2'),
    ),
    VerticalDivider(),
    Container(
      child: Text('Item3'),
    ),
  ],
)
```
