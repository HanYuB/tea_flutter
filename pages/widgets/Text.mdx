---
title: Text
---

A run of text with a single style.

## Example

```dart liveslice=Text
Text(
  'Hello, Bob! How are you?',
  textAlign: TextAlign.center,
  overflow: TextOverflow.ellipsis,
  style: TextStyle(fontWeight: FontWeight.bold),
)
```
