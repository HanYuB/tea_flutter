title: SelectableText
---

A run of selectable text with a single style.

`youtube: https://youtu.be/ZSU3ZXOs6hc`

## Example

```dart liveslice=SelectableText
SelectableText(
  'Hello! How are you?',
  textAlign: TextAlign.center,
  style: TextStyle(fontWeight: FontWeight.bold),
)

```
