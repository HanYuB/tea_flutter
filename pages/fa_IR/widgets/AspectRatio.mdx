---
title: AspectRatio
---

A widget that attempts to size the child to a specific aspect ratio.

`youtube: https://youtu.be/XcnP3_mO_Ms`

## Example

```dart liveslice=AspectRatio
Container(
  height: 200,
  child: new AspectRatio(
    aspectRatio: 4 / 3,
    child: new Container(
      color: Colors.red,
    ),
  ),
)
```
